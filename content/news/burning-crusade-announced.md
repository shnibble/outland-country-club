The Burning Crusade Classic expansion is officially happening in 2021! 

No official release date is set yet. The rumors leaked a few months ago were that it would drop in May of 2021 but Blizzard has not confirmed (or denied) that.

Regardless, we now know for *certain* that the Dark Portal will be opening and we can progress our characters into the BC expansion! Much hype!
