Out with the old and in with the new: 7 (mostly) fresh officers have been voted on and confirmed to their positions to launch our ventures into TBC.

* Lawfulgood will retain his position as the Guild Master.
* Barrabus (Bart) will take on the role of Guild Administrator.
* Shioh will be returning to officership as the Guild Banker.
* Zanth will take on the role of Tanks Officer.
* Marianna is stepping into an officer position as the new Healers Officer.
* Stifle is also stepping up for the first time to be the Fighters Officer.
* And finally Shadeeba and Kiizuu will be working together to co-lead the casters as Casters Officers.

Welcome aboard everyone!
